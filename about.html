Miraculous Beginnings

The Lotcoin team is composed of highly skilled and motivated individuals from all over the globe. They were brought together by a series of fantastically opportune events, and are united under a shared vision for the future of finance and entertainment. In mid-March, 2021, twelve hopeful investors in a new cryptocurrency project joined a community server.
The uniquely talented twelve were bound to find each other and realize their opportunity to “capture lightning in a bottle" by starting their own project together. The lotcoin vision was put into action as quickly as it was conceived of, and the rest is history.
